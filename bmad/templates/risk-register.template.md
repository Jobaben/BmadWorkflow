## Escalation Criteria

Risks should be escalated when:
- Score reaches 6 or higher
- Mitigation is not effective
- New information increases probability/impact
- Risk is about to materialize

**Escalation Path**:
1. Project Lead
2. Technical Lead
3. Stakeholder

## Appendix

### Risk Assessment Guidance

**Probability Assessment**:
- Low: < 30% chance of occurring
- Medium: 30-60% chance of occurring
- High: > 60% chance of occurring

**Impact Assessment**:
- Low: Minimal impact, easily absorbed
- Medium: Noticeable impact, requires response
- High: Severe impact, threatens project success
